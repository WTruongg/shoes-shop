Learn how to lace up Vans Authetic shoes

03/04/2022  Admin



The way to tie Vans Authentic shoes is quite diverse and easy to do, so it is used by most of you every day to bring something new to your Vans shoes. Besides, changing the way you tie your shoelaces also helps you feel excited and confident to unleash your creativity for your everyday style. Let Drake VN pocket more unique ways to tie Vans shoelaces right in the article below! Surely it will help you find the most impressive way to tie your own.


Source: Collector

How to tie Vans Authentic shoelaces in a trapezoidal pattern

Of the many ways to tie Vans Authentic shoes, the trapezoidal lace-up method is quite popular with many of you. Because this way of tying is quite simple and easy to do, but it makes a difference and is much more impressive than normal knotting styles. With 4 basic steps, you can tie a unique trapezoidal pattern for your Vans Authentic shoes. Let's get started right away with these 4 steps:


Source: Collector

Step 1: Thread the two ends of the string into the top first hole in the direction from outside to inside. Pull evenly to balance the two ropes.
Step 2: Take the left shoelace and insert it into the next hole on the same side from the inside out. At the same time, the right string is pierced into the third hole in the same row, also from the inside out.
Step 3: Continue to thread the left wire through the right into the opposite hole, from the outside to the inside. Do the same with the right string.
Step 4: With the remaining piercing holes, you do the same as step 2.3. However, the right strand goes into the next hole, and the left strand goes into the 3rd hole in the same row. At the end of the row, you can tie and hide the shoelaces under the tongue and you've finished tying the trapezoidal Vans Authentic shoelaces.


Source: Collector

How to lace Vans Authentic Loosely style shoes

Loose shoelaces always make people feel annoyed and uncomfortable. Therefore, the correct way of tying shoelaces to limit the slippage of shoelaces is also something that people are quite concerned about. Loosely style Vans Authentic shoelace without laces, less slip on shoelaces than other styles will definitely make you satisfied. Get started right away with the following 4 basic steps:


Source: Collector

Step 1: Thread the rope through the first two holes on the top in the direction from the inside out.
Step 2: Take the right piece of string and put it in the opposite hole on the left, and vice versa. Then continue to take the right part of the wire and pierce it into the next hole and solder. The left side does the same.
Step 3: Take the right piece of string and put it through the second hole on the left again from the outside to the inside, doing the opposite of the left side of the string.
Step 4: The right part of the string is pierced into the next hole in the same row, and vice versa. Do the same steps 2 and 3. Make friends thread the two ends of the extra laces into the two holes on the last two sides of the eyelet. So you have completed how to tie Vans Authentic Loosely shoelaces.


Source: Collector

How to tie a diamond shaped Vans shoelace

With the ways of tying Vans Authentic shoes, the diamond style is considered the most unique style of lacing. Most people find this type of belt quite sophisticated, but with Vans Authentic, there are only 5-6 piercings, so it will be much simpler. Just follow these 4 basic steps and you can complete this knot.


Source: Collector

Step 1: Thread the 2 ends of the wire into the first 2 holes in the direction from the inside to the outside.
Step 2: Then put the string on the left side into the 3rd hole in the opposite row. Do the same with the right string. Continue to put the left string through the 2nd hole in the same row, the right side does the same.
Step 3: Thread the left string into the opposite 4th hole. Then thread the right wire down the first part of the wire and put it into the 4th hole in the left row.
Step 4: Continue to put 2 pieces of wire on the upper hole and do the same steps 1,2,3. In a row, you can tie the string and hide it under the reed. So you've done how to tie the Vans Authentic shoelace with diamond pattern.


Source: Collector

How to tie Vans Authentic shoelaces Zig Zag

Zig Zag style Vans Authentic shoelaces are always attracted by the playful, younger look of your shoes. This style is also quite simple and easy to do quickly so you can use it for daily changes. Shake hands now with 4 simple steps to change "new shirt" for your Vans Authentic shoes.


Source: Collector

Step 1: Thread the 2 ends of the shoelaces into the first two holes and measure the lengths of the two laces to be equal.
Step 2: Take the left shoelace and insert it into the second hole on the same side, from the outside to the inside. Then continue to pierce the 2nd hole on the opposite side from the inside out. Continue through the third hole on the right side. Continue doing this in a zigzag pattern until the last piercing.
Step 3: Take the right shoelace and thread it under the left laces in the shape of a Zigzag. Continuously thread to the last eyelet and you have a Zigzag-shaped shoelace.
Step 4: Tie the string and hide under the reed. Finished the unique and easy way to tie Vans Authentic shoes.
