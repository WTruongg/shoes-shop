The "heart-stopping" color scheme of Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas

03/04/2022   Admin


Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas has brought an extremely impressive version for its fans. Besides, this return Converse will bring a new experience to users with special Recycled RPET Canvas material. Let's discover with FootwareFactory that there are special things hidden in this design.

Recycled RPET Canvas - Recycle to make life more green

Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas collection has been improved with quite unique materials. People may not know that the R in RPET means recycled, PET is called polyethylene terephthalate. Or more simply, you can understand that it is an environmentally friendly recycled fabric. This material is mainly used in industry, clothing production or home textiles.

So what is so special about this material that the designers brought into the new Converse collection? First of all, it can be said that the ability to limit water absorption is very good, very suitable as the main material for shoes. The thicker the fabric, the higher its ability to limit water absorption, so if it encounters water, it is likely that the water will slide off the surface of the fabric.

Source: Collector

The second advantage that this material brings is its high heat resistance. So, if used as the main material for shoes, when in a high temperature environment, the shoes will not lose their form or dry out. Besides, RPET fabric is highly resistant to tearing, so it will last you longer. With the above advantages, it will help users feel more comfortable and comfortable when wearing Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas.

Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas with impressive color scheme

Midnight Navy - Exquisite, unique creates a luxurious look

Midnight Navy - The "hard-wearing" color scheme of guys or girls who like warmth and simplicity but still make their own mark. With this tone, you can easily mix-match with different styles. When combined with the Midnight Navy color scheme, you just need to pay attention to the intensity of the color shades in your outfit so that it is flexible. Along with that, this blue color also increases your new day energy, choose to combine with blue or white colors to look more prominent wherever you are.

Papyrus - Color schemes dominate this year's fashion market trends

Not too strange for fashionistas, the Papyrus / Egret / Black color scheme seems to have become a trend in recent years. The Papyrus color scheme seems to have brought a new "color" to the Converse Chuck 1970s High Recycled Canvas. With this neutral color, you can combine it with light or dark colors depending on your preferences, to create your own personality. Not only that, this color is also suitable for many ages and is easy to coordinate with many different outfits. But to make it more prominent, you should use contrasting colors such as black, blue, .. to help enhance the unique impression of your own shoes.

Pink – The sweetness that dominates every outfit

Sweet and delicate is what Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas brings into its color scheme this time. Psychologists have shown that mood and emotions will be affected by the surrounding colors. Converse Recycles RPET Canvas is covered in pink throughout the entire shoe, giving you enthusiasm and positive energy for the whole day.

If you are looking to impress your partner on a first date, pink shoes will be the “must have item” for you. Try changing your everyday look with Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas to make a difference to your style.

What's impressive about the design of Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas?

Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas collection still owns two high neck and low neck designs, more suitable for your choice. If High Top makes your outfit unique and personal with a high neckline that hugs your ankles, in Low Top you will feel more flexible and dynamic for your steps. Regardless of the design, the wearer feels the classic charm and comfort of this collection.

Bringing in the classic and sophistication of the cult 1970s line, Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas has become more attractive and unique than previous designs. At the same time, the details on this item are also applied the most modern technology to give customers a great experience.

With impressive RPET Canvas material, the shoe form becomes thicker. This material also helps the wearer avoid hot weather when using shoes for a long time, limiting water absorption and providing greater durability than conventional materials. The ivory-white soleplate typical of the 1970s is thick and covered with a glossy outer layer to limit staining as well as make it easier for the wearer to clean.

Source: Collector

The distinctive black foxing around the sole creates an eye-catching highlight. Not to forget the Vintage logo on the back of the shoe. With a gentle 1970s font on "Converse All Star Chuck Taylor", white text on a black background will make the heel more fancy and attractive.

Source: Collector

A small point that people pay little attention to in this design is that the part in the reed is added with a star symbolizing the brand logo located right on the information tape. Designers are always clever to add brand identity in the most subtle and meticulous way.

Source: Collector

Not only that, the inside of the Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas shoes also has a smooth, modern OrthoLite cushioning that provides support for every step as well as contributes to reducing friction between the toes and the shoe. Modern but not harmful is what Converse designers always bring to their fans.

Source: Collector

With each new collection, the designers always put their heart into giving the wearer the best experience. Converse 70s Recycled RPET Canvas collection promises to help you change your look to be sweet and impressive with any outfit.
