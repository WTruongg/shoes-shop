Visa and Mastercard are widely accepted worldwide. If an establishment uses credit cards, your Visa or Mastercard is best. American Express and Discover also have an international presence, but they are accepted by fewer merchants.

How will i know how much my order will cost?
Just add items to your basket and click "secure check out". Borderfree calculates and displays the order total in your preferred currency, including all shipping costs. The order total presented at checkout is the exact amount you’ll be billed by Borderfree, and there will be no additional costs due upon delivery.
What happens after the order has been placed?
After submitting your payment details, you will receive an order confirmation by email. Shipping times are mentioned on the Shoe Factory homepage and will appear on your order confirmation. Once your order is shipped, you will receive an official invoice.
Can I cancel my order?
You can cancel physical items or orders that haven't entered the shipping process yet.
Will I be able to change my address after the order is confirmed?
Buyers can now choose to change the address after placing an order before sellers proceed to arrange for shipment. If the order has been shipped, buyers are advised to inform sellers regarding the changes at the soonest and may need to cancel the order to place a new order with the correct address.
The password reset is not working. What should I do?
If your new password isn’t working, please request the password reset once again. Ensure consistency with spelling and capitalization when you set the password and log in later. It could be that you previously used different upper/lowercase letters or that you added blank spaces.
How do I change my e-mail address?
Here's how to do it:
1. Open account and click the gear icon.
2. Select Settings.
3. Select the “Forwarding and POP/IMAP” tab.
4. Click on “Add a forwarding address.”
5. Enter your new email address.
6. You'll get a verification email at your new email address—click the link to confirm.
How do I create an account?
1. Select Accounts.
2. Tap on Add account.
3. Pick Create account.
4. Follow on-screen instructions by entering your personal information, selecting a username, etc...
5. Tap the I Agree button to create your account.

How long does it take to ship my order?
Domestic orders normally arrive within 3-7 days of shipping, unless otherwise noted.
International orders normally arrive within 2-4 weeks of shipping. Please note that these orders need to pass through the customs office in your country before it will be released for final delivery, which can occasionally cause additional delays.
Can I combine shipping on two or more orders?
Simplify shipping to a single buyer in a marketplace by combining their orders into one shipment . Orders page . Select the orders to combine by checking the box to the left of the individual orders or to the left of Order Date to select all . Then select the Combine Selected Orders.
Will I pay taxes for international shipping?
How you pay duty depends on how your goods were shipped. If your goods were shipped through the International Postal Service, you will need to pay the mail carrier and/or go to your local post office to pay any duty and processing fees owed when your package arrives at that post office.
